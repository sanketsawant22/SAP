# 📘 SAP SD Notes - Lesson 3

# 🔗 Definition vs Assignment + Plant & Storage Location

> ⭐ **This is the lesson where most beginners finally "get" SAP.**

---

# 🤔 First, understand one thing...

When you opened **SPRO**, you saw two sections:

✅ **Definition**

✅ **Assignment**

Many people memorize the steps without understanding the difference.

Let's fix that.

---

# 🏗️ What is Definition?

Definition simply means:

> **"Create the object."**

Imagine you're opening a new company.

Before assigning employees, you first need to create the company.

Same in SAP.

You first create:

* 🏢 Company Code
* 🛒 Sales Organization
* 🛍 Distribution Channel
* 📱 Division
* 🏬 Sales Office
* 👨‍💼 Sales Group
* 📦 Shipping Point

At this point...

SAP only knows these objects exist.

❌ They are **not connected**.

---

### 🎯 Real Life Example

Imagine this...

You have built:

🏢 Office in Pune

🏢 Office in Mumbai

🏢 Office in Delhi

They exist.

But...

Nobody knows which company owns them.

Exactly the same in SAP.

---

# 🔗 What is Assignment?

Assignment means:

> **Connect two existing objects.**

Think of plugging devices together.

```text
Created ❌

Company Code

Sales Organization

Plant
```

↓

Assignment

```text
Company Code
      │
      ▼
Sales Organization
```

Now SAP understands:

👉 "This Sales Organization belongs to this Company."

---

# 🎯 Example

Suppose you created:

```text
Company Code

Y100
```

and

```text
Sales Organization

Y452
```

They exist separately.

Now Assignment says

```text
Assign

Y100

↓

Y452
```

Now SAP understands

> Jack Organization belongs to Jack Company.

---

# 🧠 Memory Trick

## Definition

👉 Create

## Assignment

👉 Connect

That's it.

---

# 🏭 Plant

Now let's learn another important object.

---

## 💡 What is Plant?

A Plant is a physical location.

It can be:

🏭 Factory

🏢 Manufacturing Unit

📦 Warehouse

Distribution Center

---

### Example

Jack Electronics has

```text
Pune Factory

Nagpur Factory

Delhi Factory
```

Each is a separate Plant.

Example

```text
Plant

Y100

Jack Pune Plant
```

---

## 🧠 Remember

> Plant = Where products exist.

---

# 📦 Storage Location

Inside one Plant...

There are many storage areas.

Example

```text
Pune Plant

│

├── Raw Material

├── Finished Goods

├── Returned Goods

└── Spare Parts
```

Each is a Storage Location.

---

### Example

```text
Plant

Pune

↓

Storage Location

0001

Finished Goods
```

Another

```text
0002

Raw Material
```

---

# 🧠 Remember

> Plant = Building

> Storage Location = Room inside the building

---

# 🎯 Real Life Example

Imagine Amazon.

Amazon has

🏢 Warehouse Pune

Inside

📦 Electronics

📦 Clothing

📦 Books

📦 Furniture

Warehouse = Plant

Sections = Storage Locations

---

# ⭐ Difference

| Plant 🏭                             | Storage Location 📦     |
| ------------------------------------ | ----------------------- |
| Large physical unit                  | Small area inside Plant |
| Manufacturing/Storage                | Specific stock area     |
| One Plant has many Storage Locations | Belongs to one Plant    |

---

# 🔗 Typical Assignment

After defining Plant

SAP asks

Assign

```text
Company Code

↓

Plant
```

Meaning

Which company owns this Plant?

---

Later

```text
Plant

↓

Storage Location
```

Now SAP knows

Storage belongs to this Plant.

# 🤔 Why do we need both Plant and Sales Organization?

Excellent question.

Imagine a customer buys a laptop.

Sales Organization

✔ Takes the order

↓

Plant

✔ Provides the product

↓

Shipping Point

✔ Ships the product

↓

Customer

---

Think like this:

🛒 Sales Organization = Sales Team

🏭 Plant = Warehouse/Factory

📦 Shipping Point = Dispatch Gate

---

# 💡 Easy Story

Imagine you're buying an iPhone.

You go to Apple Store.

The salesperson creates the order.

Who did that?

🛒 Sales Organization

Now the warehouse checks stock.

Who has stock?

🏭 Plant

Then packing starts.

Where?

📦 Shipping Point

Truck leaves.

Customer receives order.

Done.
